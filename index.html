📺 How to Add Tip Daddy to Your Stream

  • Open your Chaturbate broadcast dashboard and navigate to the Apps tab.
  • Search for Tip Daddy and click install.
  • Enable all permissions when prompted — especially Broadcast Panel Access.
  • Customize your Goal Value, Initial Crown Cost, and Time Step in settings.
  • That’s it! When your broadcast starts, Tip Daddy will auto-run and begin tracking.

📘 Commands & Examples

  • /reset – Clears all session data, including the crown and progress toward the goal. Use if your stream restarts or you want to begin fresh mid-show.
  • /clearalltime – Permanently clears all-time leaderboard data. This cannot be undone!
  • /setalltime username tokens – Manually set an all-time tip record (e.g. /setalltime CoolGuy123 2000).
  • /menu – Show your current tip menu, including timers and token costs.
  • /setmenu [item name] [tokens] – Adds a tip menu item.
  • /setmenu [item name] [tokens] [minutes] – Adds a timed menu item that shows a countdown in the panel.
  • /removeitem [item name] – Removes an item from your tip menu (case-insensitive).
  • /clearmenu – Clears all menu items.

✨ Features in Action

  • Crown System: Users tip to take the Daddy crown. If someone tips more than the current Daddy, they steal the title. Chat displays a dethrone message like:
    🔥 New Daddy! CoolGuy123 just took the crown from King69 with a 700 tip!
  • All-Time Rankings: If a user beats their personal best, their all-time record is updated. If they enter the Top 5, they appear on the leaderboard and may earn the All-Time Daddy spot.
  • Broadcast Panel: Your current goal progress, session Daddy, and all-time top tipper are shown on screen to hype up the room.
  • Timed Menu Items: You can set custom tip rewards that start a timer. For example:
    🕒 5-Minute Tease Show – 250 tokens will launch a visible countdown in the broadcast panel.
  • Private Feedback: Non-Daddies who tip get a private message like:
    You're just 75 tokens away from taking the crown! 👑
  • Repeating Messages: The app can remind users who's Daddy and how many tokens are needed to take the lead.
